# Citation

## How to cite EPM

**Model reference**

> Chattopadhyay, D., De Sisternes, F., Oguah, S. K. W. (2018). *World Bank Electricity Planning Model (EPM) Mathematical Formulation*. Energy Sector Management Assistance Program (ESMAP), World Bank, Washington DC.

**Software**

> ESMAP — World Bank (2016–present). *EPM: Electricity Planning Model*. [https://github.com/ESMAP-World-Bank-Group/EPM](https://github.com/ESMAP-World-Bank-Group/EPM). DOI: [10.5281/zenodo.15591290](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.15591290)

Previous versions are archived on Zenodo: [zenodo.org/communities/esmap-epm](https://zenodo.org/communities/esmap-epm)
